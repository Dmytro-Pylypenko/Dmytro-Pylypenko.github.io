  • Seasons 6

Vikings is a swanky historical series, one of the best in its genre, the show boasts a huge fan base, as well as sparking interest in history and reviving the viking fashion.

"Vikings" was created by order of the American channel History, however, Canadians and Irish people worked on it. The series strikes with large-scale battle scenes, a solid and deep script, as well as maximum attention to the historical era. The authors of the series spent a lot of time and money (the budget of the first season is $ 40 million, then the amounts increased) to make the Vikings and their contemporaries look in the show as they were in their time - VIII-IX centuries AD. On the other hand, the lack of accurate historical facts and the (semi) legendary nature of many events allowed the authors of the series to freely interpret many events.
