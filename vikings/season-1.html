01 Season

The series introduces us to the brutal and mysterious world of Ragnar Lothbrok, a Viking warrior and farmer who is eager to explore — and raid — the distant shores of the ocean. His ambition puts him at odds with the local chieftain, Count Haraldson, who insists on sending his raiders to the poor East, not the unknown West. When Ragnar teams up with his shipbuilder friend Floki to create a new generation of fearless ships capable of conquering the rough northern seas, the scene is ready for conflict.

But for all their war and bloodshed, the Vikings are also a story of family and brotherhood, capturing love and affection between Ragnar and his wife Lagertha, a fierce warrior. This is the story of Ragnar's brother Rollo, a fierce fighter who is seething with jealousy; about Count Haraldson's wife Ziggy, an obedient beauty who may not be very faithful; and about the monk Athelstan, whose Christian morality contradicts the pagan Viking society. When ambition and innovation shake civilization, these characters will be put to the test — and their lifestyles will never be the same.
